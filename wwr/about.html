About Us

History

White Knuckle Rafting is a family owned and operated business that has been providing unforgettable experiences for over 25 years. We are dedicated to providing a safe and exciting adventure for all of our guests. Our guides are experienced and knowledgeable about the river and the surrounding area. We offer trips for all skill levels, from beginners to experts. We are committed to providing the best experience possible for our guests. Come join us for an adventure of a lifetime!
